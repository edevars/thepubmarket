## Description

Move from single anchor seller to vetted, admin-invited multi-seller marketplace: generalized Stripe Connect onboarding, seller portal access control, identity/session hardening, and anti-bot protection. No public self-registration. Non-custodial invariant must be preserved in all Connect/payout work.
